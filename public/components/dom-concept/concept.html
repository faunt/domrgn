Engagement levels

Tailor experience for users at various levels of engagement with the product, and in community itself.

Emphasis on Level 1 experience. Aim for compelling product proposition for least engaged. Expect this to be largest user segment.

Level 1

Barely there. First time user, but also almost everyone else. Low attention and interest in product. More likely to ignore then contribute.

Level 2

Still only consuming rather than producing content. May be willing to personalise in some way, e.g. sign in, filter for interests

Level 3

Passive participation
